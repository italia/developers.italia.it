Contribute

Do you want to help but don’t know where to start?

All the development is hosted on GitHub @italia where you can find our projects, as well as technologies they use.

To easily find what you are interested in, click on “Contribute!” button and find out all the open issues waiting for contribution.

Contribute! Explore GitHub Italia

Contact us

Have you developed new integrations, libraries or connectors for languages ​​or technologies that are not currently present? Contact us to bring them to Developers Italia and get in touch in real time with other developers!

Contact us on Slack and Matrix

Participate in development of public software

The catalogue of Developers Italia includes the open source software used or released by the Public Administration. Everyone can contribute: find bugs and security issues, or propose improvements and new features. Public digital services can now benefit from your contribution too.

Explore the catalogue

Add public software to the catalogue

Help us populate the catalogue by discovering software released as open source by the Public Administration, or third-party open source software targeted at the public sector: by helping the maintainers to add a publiccode.yml to their repositories you will let the Developer Italia crawler discover them.

Participate

Enter forum.italia.it to discuss the public digital services: platforms, API, software, security and more. Join the discussion!

Chat and collaborate in real time with the maintainers and the other developers in the Slack and Matrix server of Developers Italia.

In github.com/italia we develop (together) SDKs and examples for national platforms.
